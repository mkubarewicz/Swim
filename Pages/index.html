About

This website is to create a space on the internet for all things to do with swimming.

You can search through a collection of swimming competition times to see how you rank on the "Times" page. You can add your own times to the collection by going to the "Compete" page and creating an account. You can go to the "Training" page and see instructional videos on swimming, tips, and practice sets you can try on your own. Happy swimming!
