Adventures in a Public Pool

January 1, 2014 by Maggie

Sometimes public pools aren't that fun. More later. If people just learned to splash less. Splashing makes you go slower. Splashing also gets us wet. Sometimes we don't want a mouthful of water that came from your feet. Not that your feet aren't nice. It's just sometimes air is where air should go and water is where water should go. This will be coherent at a later time. Maybe. Probably. Most likely?

I Used to Blog More

December 14, 2013 by Maggie

One day I will be better at blogging. But not today. Today is not blog day. Today is design day. BACK TO DESIGNING NOW. No wait, this post is too small. How will I know what it looks like? I want it to be a real post's size. But I don't think I can write that much nonsense right now. Usually I'm better at writing nonsense than this. But today I just feel tired. Today I went swimming. But I kind of regret it. Because now I am sore. And that is what happens when you swim. Plus my ears are filled with water. And my nose. And my eyes are red. And I keep sneezing everywhere. I think I'm allergic to pools. How saddening.

About

This blog is dedicated to Maggie and Karen's adventures in swimming. Maggie has been swimming since she was a little girl and used to swim competitively (VERY SLOWLY). Karen's perspective is from someone newer to swimming. They talk to each other about the weird events in the pool. Such as texting. Texting and swimming do not mix. Do not do it.
